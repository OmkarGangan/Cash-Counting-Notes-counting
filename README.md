# Cash-Counting-Notes-counting
Program to count notes required in user entered Amount.

Output:

Enter Amount:705

1 note of 500
1 note of 200
1 coin of 5

Enter Amount:5349
To pay ₹5349/- you will require: 
2 notes of 2000
1 note of 1000
1 note of 200
1 note of 100
2 notes of 20
1 coin of 5
2 coins of 2
